*_TIME & SCHEDULING_*

*_HIRING_*
    • <https://www.notion.so/Disallow-Last-Minute-Rescheduling-31aa1747bfd1804c9c44ef33afe25587|Disallow Last-Minute Rescheduling> // Fully Released  (2026-03-03)
        ◦ *What:* Disallow applicants to reschedule their interview 1 hour before the interview starts (they can still cancel the interview).
        ◦ *Why It Matters:* Help our customers reduce the occurrence of last-minute rescheduling, which is hard to accommodate and also takes up other availabilities from their calendar.
        ◦ *Owner of Feature:* Derek Dong

        ◦ *What:* Let users save frequently used messages and reuse them when messaging applicants.
        ◦ *Why It Matters:* Greatly improve our users’ messaging efficiency when dealing with a big volume of applications.
        ◦ *Owner of Feature:* Derek Dong
    • <https://www.notion.so/VoiceAI-candidate-helper-311a1747bfd180b2965ac166cf709710|VoiceAI candidate helper> // Partially Released  (2026-02-25)

🔵 *COMING SOON:*
*_HIRING_*
    • <https://www.notion.so/Detect-possible-duplicate-applications-30ba1747bfd1815d9496e273e9a465c4|Detect possible duplicate applications> // Coming Soon
        ◦ *What:* Currently we only use mobile number to detect duplicate applications. But we've seen applicants re-apply with different mobile numbers. So we will use their full name and email to detect whether there are any possible duplicate applications. Users can then compare their profile and determine whether they're from the same person or not.
        ◦ *Why It Matters:* This helps saving the manual work of searching for names and emails to learn if the applicant has applied or been rejected before.
        ◦ *Owner of Feature:* Derek Dong
    • <https://www.notion.so/Let-applicants-pick-a-specific-interviewer-30ba1747bfd181698146f0c3aa0528c0|Let applicants pick a specific interviewer> // Coming Soon
        ◦ *What:* When letting applicant choose from interview leads' calendar, if there are multiple interview leads configured in the hiring process settings, users will have the ability to let applicant choose only from a specific interviewer's calendar.
        ◦ *Why It Matters:* This is particular useful when only certain interviewer can speak Spanish (or other language), or when they intentionally want dedicated person to be the interviewer.
        ◦ *Owner of Feature:* Derek Dong
